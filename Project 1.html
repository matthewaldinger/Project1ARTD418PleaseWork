Bird House

Pizza Succeeds despite Challenges

On Saturday, March 7th, Champaign's longest running DIY venue, Bird House, hosted the first PizzaFM show in over two years. Local newbies in the DIY music scene Soft and Dumb opened the show with their star gazed guitars and dreamy vocals. The show then went on to feature four touring acts: Superkick, a midwest emo band that loosened the crowd up with their headbanging melodies, Other Suns, Chicago indie rock band that brought the dance floor out as well as the groovy tunes, Dog Mom, another Chicago based indie rock band that substitutes groovy for raw energy and soul, and The Hazy Seas that ended the night with showering guitar riffs and drowning harmonies. Though there was worry of having four touring bands on the bill, the overall reception of the show was a rocking success.
